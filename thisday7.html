7.23.10

An important day not to forget in world history. July, 1991. Chill Wave. All noted rappers and producers were locked into a country music hot box and exterminated. This tragedy brought about a new Don Juan meets Pedro Paramo style of music. People were celebrating the massacre at the clubs for a month afterward. MJ wrote a song called Blood On The Dancefloor in homage. The spiritual cleansing supplied us with gangster rap, but the nation seems to try hard to forget about this mass execution. Char-Broiled Rapper Month.
